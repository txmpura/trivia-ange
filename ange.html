Did You Know? Fascinating facts about UST Angelicum College

1

Non-Graded System

UST Angelicum was one of the first schools in the Philippines to adopt a non-graded system, meaning students progress based on mastery rather than traditional grade levels.

2

Founded by a Dominican Priest

The school was established in 1972 by Fr. Rogelio B. Alarcon, O.P., a Dominican priest who wanted a more flexible and individualized approach to education.

3

Connection to the Vatican's Angelicum University

The name "Angelicum" comes from the Pontifical University of Saint Thomas Aquinas (Angelicum) in Rome, a prestigious Dominican university where many Filipino priests, including some UST professors, have studied.

4

Sarah Geronimo's Alma Mater

Pop superstar Sarah Geronimo studied here before transferring to another school. She remains one of its most famous alumni.

5

No Numerical Grades Until College

In its basic education program, students receive descriptive evaluations (like "Proficient" or "Advancing") instead of traditional number grades. Numerical grades only appear in college.

6

Bianca Bustamante, F1 Academy Racer

Bianca Bustamante, the first Filipina to race in the F1 Academy (a Formula 1 women's racing series), is an alumna of UST Angelicum.

7

Formerly an All-Boys School

When it first opened in 1972, Angelicum was an all-boys school. It later became co-educational.

8

UST Affiliation (But Independent Financially)

Despite being integrated with UST Manila in 2018, Angelicum still manages its own finances and operations, retaining its unique identity.

9

No School Uniform Policy (Before)

Unlike most Catholic schools in the Philippines, Angelicum did not require uniforms for many years, promoting a more relaxed environment. However, policies may have changed since integration with UST.

10

Home of the "Tigercubs" & "Roebucks"

The school's mascots are the Tigercubs (for younger students) and Roebucks (for older students), inspired by the Dominican tradition of using animals as symbols.

About UST Angelicum College

Full Name: University of Santo Tomas – Angelicum College (formerly Angelicum School and Angelicum College).

Type: Private, non-profit, non-graded Catholic basic and higher education institution.

Religious Affiliation: Roman Catholic (Dominican Order).

Motto: Caritas, Justitia, Fortitudo (Charity, Justice, Fortitude).

Established: July 1972 (founded by Dominican priest Rev. Fr. Rogelio B. Alarcon, OP).

Location: Quezon City, Metro Manila, Philippines.

Campus: Urban.

Integration with UST

In 2018, it was renamed UST Angelicum College after integrating with the University of Santo Tomas (UST).

A new Board of Trustees was formed (9 members from UST Manila, 3 from Angelicum).

Retains administrative and financial independence.

UST Rector serves as Chief Executive Officer, while Angelicum's former rector became Chief Operating Officer.
